Our projects

In the more than four years we have managed a large number of individual projects in the various corporate divisions. The spectrum ranges from smaller projects to complex realizations. Below you will find some of our projects as well as a brief overview of some of our customers for whom we have implemented projects.

Skill: Nintendo Switch

The Nintendo Switch is a video game console made by the Japanese company Nintendo. As a so-called hybrid console, it can be used both as a stationary console, i.e. one connected to an external screen, and as a portable handheld console (source: Wikipedia). The (still) unofficial "Nintendo Switch" Amazon Alexa Skill allows users to ask for the latest update for the game console or Nintendo Switch games to get information about it. You can also easily play Nintendo music.
We are currently working on this skill!

Coming soon!

Skill: Quick, Draw!

"Quick Draw!" is a browser game developed by Google LLC in which the player has the task of drawing a sketch of a randomly selected object or word within 19 seconds. Meanwhile, an artificial intelligence (AI) of an artificial neural network (ANN) tries to guess what the drawing is supposed to represent as quickly as possible. The game is based on deep learning, a sub-area of machine learning (source: Wikipedia). The (unofficial) Amazon Alexa Skill launches this application on Amazon Echo devices with a screen.
We are currently working on this skill!

Coming soon!
